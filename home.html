    Kazuki Takahashi


    Kazuki Takahashi was a visionary Japanese manga artist best known as the creator of Yu-Gi-Oh!. Launched in 1996, Yu-Gi-Oh! combined storytelling with strategic gameplay, capturing fans worldwide. Takahashi's work not only led to a popular manga and anime but also inspired one of the best-selling trading card games ever created.
